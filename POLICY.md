# PackArcade2 Policies

**Last Updated: March 18, 2026**

## 1. 📜 Acceptable Use Policy

### ✅ Allowed Content
- Original games and websites
- Educational content
- Personal projects
- Open source software
- Creative coding experiments

### ❌ Prohibited Content
- Illegal content or activities
- Malware or malicious code
- Copyrighted material without permission
- Hate speech or harassment
- Phishing or scam sites
- Crypto mining scripts
- Anything that harms the platform or users

## 2. 🔒 Privacy Policy

### Data We Collect
- Username and email (if provided)
- Projects and files you create
- Usage analytics (anonymous)

### Data We DON'T Collect
- Passwords (we don't have login yet!)
- Payment information
- Personal addresses
- Sensitive personal data

### Data Storage
- All data stored on our servers in D:\PackArcade
- Backups stored offline on USB drives
- We never sell your data

## 3. ⚖️ Copyright Policy

### DMCA Compliance
If you believe your copyrighted work has been infringed:

1. Email support@packarcade.win with:
   - Identification of copyrighted work
   - URL of infringing content
   - Your contact information
   - Statement of good faith belief

2. We'll review and respond within 48 hours

### Repeat Infringers
Users with valid DMCA takedowns will be warned. Repeat offenders may lose access.

## 4. 🎮 Game Upload Policy

### EXE Games
- Must be DOS/classic games only
- No modern Windows executables
- You must have rights to distribute
- No malware or viruses

### HTML Games
- Must be your original work
- No iframe embedding of other sites
- No crypto miners or malicious scripts

## 5. 🏗️ Project Hosting Policy

### Websites
- Must be appropriate for all ages
- No commercial hosting without permission
- No excessive resource usage
- Backend processes limited to 1 per project

### Resource Limits
- CPU: Fair usage (we'll monitor)
- Memory: 256MB per process
- Storage: 100MB per project (for now)
- Bandwidth: 1GB/month per project

## 6. 📧 Email Policy

- **support@packarcade.win**: For bugs and issues
- **hello@packarcade.win**: General inquiries
- **personal@packarcade.win**: Spam welcome! Send memes, jokes, anything non-threatening

We actually read personal@packarcade.win - it's our community inbox!

## 7. 🔐 Security Policy

### Reporting Vulnerabilities
Found a security issue? Email support@packarcade.win with:
- Description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact

We'll respond within 24 hours and work to fix it ASAP.

### Responsible Disclosure
Please give us reasonable time to fix issues before public disclosure.

## 8. 🗑️ Data Retention

- Active projects: Kept indefinitely
- Inactive projects (6+ months): May be archived
- Deleted projects: Removed from active storage within 30 days
- Backups: Retained for 30 days

THE SOFTWARE IS PROVIDED "AS IS", WITHOUT WARRANTY OF ANY KIND. We're students running this for fun - use at your own risk!
